About Moj

Our brand, MOJ method is from Prishtina, Kosovo. Moj expresses a feminine exclamation that reinforces the expression of the call, leaving you with a feeling that lasts.

Our mission is offering women of the 21-century the essence of a bold style and timeless allure.

MOJ expresses the power of ones wardrobe to shape and express identity to fuel and satisfy desire. Its rich textures, fluid proportions and nuanced neutral palette push ease to elegant new extremes. The clothes instantly add a super feminine touch to your look, just throw it on, add a pair of heels and your statement styling is done.
